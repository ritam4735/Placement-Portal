About the Portal

One platform for the entire placement cycle

From student registration to final selection — every step is streamlined, tracked, and transparent for all three roles.

  • Students self-register, upload resumes, and apply for approved drives
  • Companies register, get admin approval, then create and manage drives
  • Admin oversees everything — approvals, blacklisting, and placement records
  • Full application history and status tracking for every participant
  • Duplicate application prevention enforced at database level

Capabilities

Built for real placement workflows

Resume Upload
Students securely upload PDF resumes. Companies and admins can view them directly from the portal.
Drive Management
Companies create, edit, and close placement drives. Drives go live only after admin approval.
Application Tracking
Full status pipeline: Applied → Shortlisted → Selected / Rejected. Students see their status in real time.
Role-Based Access
Admin, company, and student roles are strictly separated. Each user sees only what they need.
Smart Search & Filters
Admin can filter students by course, department, batch, semester, and CGPA for precise targeting.
Blacklist Management
Admin can block or unblock students and companies at any time, with immediate effect on portal access.
